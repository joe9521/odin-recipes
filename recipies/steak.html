Sirloin Steak, egg, and chips

An easy and quick classic, why not try steak egg and chips tonight?

Ingredients:

  • Sirloin steak
  • An egg
  • Oven chips

Instructions

  1. First, add your chips to a baking tray and place them in a preheated oven
  2. Then, add garlic butter to a frying pan and begin to heat it at a high heat
  3. Once the pan is hot add the steak and fry each side for four minutes
  4. Take the steak and add it to the plate, then quickly fry the egg in the hot pan
  5. Add the chips from the oven
